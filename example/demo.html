Data display

Drag a rectangle on the diagram to zoom (click to reset). The sliders set the diagram domain and the comfort zone (shown as the green polygon, with dashed orange/red tolerance bands). Switch the coordinate convention between classical and Glück. Enter either an altitude or a pressure — the other follows automatically (the value is shown top-right of the chart). To plot your own data, upload a .csv with three columns: time (ISO 8601), humidity [%], temperature [°C]. Meteonorm hourly .dat files (year, month, day, hour, temperature, rel. humidity) are recognised automatically. Points are coloured by season.
